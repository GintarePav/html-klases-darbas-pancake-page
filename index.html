Ingrediens for "Mardi Gras Pancakes"

  • 500 g. of flour;
  • 250ml. of milk;
  • 1 egg;
  • Minor ingredients:
    • 1 g. of salt;
    • 5 g. of sugar.

"Mardi Gras Pancakes" Batter Recepe

  1. Add two cups of flour to bowl.
  2. Add a cup of milk and one egg.
  3. Add a pinch of salt and a teaspoon of sugar.
  4. Mix until the consistency of the batter is even.
